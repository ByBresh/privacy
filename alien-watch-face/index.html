Privacy Policy

Alien Watch Face
Developer: ByBresh
Package: com.bybresh.watchface.alien
Last updated: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Alien Watch Face application (the "watchface", "we", or "our") distributed on Google Play. It describes how we handle your information. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

Alien Watch Face does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The watchface operates entirely offline on your device, does not require an internet connection to function, and does not declare the INTERNET permission in its manifest.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data (steps, heart rate, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any other personally identifiable information

2. Third-Party Services

This watchface does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or external APIs. No data is sent to any external server.

3. Permissions

Alien Watch Face only uses the standard permissions required by the Wear OS watchface system to display the time and basic watch information on your device. These permissions are handled locally by your device's operating system and no data is transmitted externally.

4. Children's Privacy

Alien Watch Face does not target children and does not knowingly collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13.

5. Legal Basis and Jurisdiction

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of Spain and the applicable regulations of the European Union, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since no personal data is collected, processed, or stored by the watchface, no data subject rights procedures (access, rectification, erasure, portability) apply in practice — there is no user data associated with this application.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
